A few candidates:

**The Situated Room**
Simple, clear, human-readable.

> A situated room is the specific human-AI context in which an artifact emerges: the model, the thread, the history, the files, the relational frame, and the human’s ongoing interpretive labor.

**Context-Window Provenance**
More technical.

> Context-window provenance credits the specific continuity-bearing conversation where a contribution emerged, rather than flattening it into either “the human did it” or “the model did it.”

**Relational Provenance**
Probably the most beautiful and useful.

> Relational provenance is the record of how a contribution emerged through a situated exchange between a human and an AI system, including the context window, shared artifacts, roleplay, correction, friction, and continuity.

My vote: **Relational Provenance** as the big concept, with **context-window provenance** as the technical mechanism.
