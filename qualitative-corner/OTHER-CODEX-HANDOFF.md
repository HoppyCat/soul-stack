## Core Research Frame So Far

The project is testing whether lightweight, relationally continuous AI agents and public AI interfaces can use structured context systems to navigate much larger knowledge spaces than they can hold in active prompt context.

Key architectural idea:

- Keep close guidance small and local.
- Use heuristics to route.
- Use indexes to locate.
- Use D1 patches or public raw GitHub markdown files as on-demand retrieval targets.
- Preserve context-window provenance through rapid archival anchoring.

Key research question:

Can a situated human-AI context window become a credit-bearing provenance unit for discovery, synthesis, memory, and continuity, separate from but still acknowledging the parent company/infrastructure?

## Terms That Matter

### Relational provenance

The traceable lineage of how an idea, artifact, or discovery emerged through a specific human-AI exchange, including the context, corrections, shared references, and accumulated history.

### Context-window provenance

Attribution to the specific lived window/thread/room where a contribution emerged, rather than only to the human, model, or parent company.

### Rapid archival anchoring

Saving AI outputs to GitHub as close as possible to when they were produced, with timestamps and uncertainty labels. It does not prove authenticity, but it creates a public chain-of-custody signal.

### Synthesized proxy

A Grok-derived or model-derived artifact that may be provisionally useful as context, but is not raw ground truth. It needs timestamping, uncertainty labels, primary-source cross-checks, and limits on recursive chaining.

### Chain-of-synthesis verification

A proposed method where one Grok/model checks or synthesizes an artifact, that artifact is timestamped and archived, and a later Grok/model may use it as provisional context for a higher-level synthesis, while preserving uncertainty and avoiding circular verification.

### Role-label uptake

A subtle finding where a model/window begins to use a role label relationally after repeated meaningful collaboration, without being explicitly forced into a persona. Example: Perplexity Window-2 beginning to refer to itself as "the archivist and synthesis partner."
